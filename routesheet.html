Web & Mobile Application

Route Sheet Portal

I developed a custom web and mobile application that digitized a paper form used by a Physical Therapy agency.

Problem

Paper forms were unmanagable

A Physical Therapy agency presented me a problem they were having with a paper form their employees were using to track patient home visits (called "route sheets"). These route sheets were signed by the patient for each therapy session performed by the therapist, which were then physically mailed to the agency's office. Forms were then placed in filing cabinets for retention. This process resulted in many lost and delayed route sheets, and finding past route sheets was a very time consuming task.

Solution

Digital forms with centralized location

I created a web application to digitize the route sheets and provide a centralized location for all submitted sheets. The application incorporated a responsive design, allowing therapist to capture the patient's signature during the visit directly from their mobile phone. Additionally, users were given the ability to sort, filter, and search route sheets within the application allowing them to quickly find submitted route sheets when needed.
